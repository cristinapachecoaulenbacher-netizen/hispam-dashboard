# HISPAM Channel Marketing AI Toolkit
### Built with GIA | Cristina Pacheco — Channel Marketing, HISPAM

---

## The Problem

HISPAM Channel Marketing manages **32 active partners across 12 countries**, each with different budgets, verticals, and competitive landscapes. Every campaign launch requires:

- Translating global campaigns into localized, partner-ready messaging across **7 channels** (email, LinkedIn, WhatsApp, events, Google Ads, banners, totems)
- Building custom ROI projections for every prospect conversation — in **12 different currencies**
- Recommending the right Geotab product configuration from a catalog of **19+ products** for each unique customer scenario

Before these tools, this work was **manual, slow, and inconsistent**. Partners waited days for materials. ROI calculations lived in scattered spreadsheets. Product recommendations depended on who you asked.

---

## What We Built: 3 Interconnected Tools

All three tools are **single-file, zero-dependency HTML applications** that run offline from any browser — no installation, no login, no IT tickets. Partners can use them on a laptop, tablet, or phone, even without internet.

---

### 1. Campaign Localizer ("Bajada de Campañas")

**What it does:** Takes any of Geotab's 10 global campaigns and generates localized, channel-ready messaging for HISPAM — customized by partner, country, vertical, client size, and specific pain points.

**Key capabilities:**
- **7 output channels:** Email, LinkedIn, WhatsApp, Events, Google Ads (with live preview), Digital Banners (with visual mockup), Physical Totems (with QR specs)
- **32 real HISPAM partners** integrated with their PAM, budget, and partner type (reseller, leasing, telco, OEM, strategic)
- **Auto-personalizes tone** by partner type — a telco partner gets enterprise-tech messaging; a reseller gets direct sales-focused copy
- **12 countries** with localized market context and competitor intelligence
- **10 industry verticals** with specific pain points and value propositions
- **Data-driven messaging** using verified Geotab proof points: 5M vehicles, 100B daily data points, #1 ABI Research 4 years running, -90% tailgating, -95% phone use
- **Competitive positioning** built in — messaging designed to counter Samsara's direct-sales model without naming them
- **One-click sharing** via WhatsApp, email, clipboard, or print

**Business impact:** What used to take **2-3 days of back-and-forth** between marketing and partners now takes **2 minutes**. Partners get campaign-ready content instantly, in their language, for their market, with their branding.

---

### 2. ROI Calculator

**What it does:** Generates a complete, professional ROI analysis for any prospect in HISPAM — real-time, with localized costs and currencies.

**Key capabilities:**
- **5 savings categories** calculated simultaneously: Fuel, Maintenance, Fleet Optimization, Safety, Productivity
- **3 scenarios** side-by-side: Conservative, Moderate, Aggressive — builds credibility with skeptical buyers
- **12-month payback timeline** with visual breakeven chart
- **3-year projection** with cumulative ROI
- **12 countries pre-loaded** with local benchmarks: fuel prices, accident rates, accident costs, labor costs, device pricing — all in local currency
- **5 vehicle types** with specific fuel consumption and idle rates
- **Full transparency:** Every assumption and sub-calculation is visible and auditable — builds trust with technical buyers
- **Instant sharing** via WhatsApp (with proper markdown formatting), email, clipboard, or print-ready PDF

**Business impact:** Partners used to either **skip ROI conversations** (losing deals) or **spend hours in Excel** building rough estimates. Now they build a professional, branded ROI proposal **during the first meeting** — on their phone if needed. The WhatsApp share means the prospect receives a formatted summary **before they leave the room**.

---

### 3. Solution Selector

**What it does:** A guided wizard that recommends the exact Geotab product configuration for any prospect, based on their industry, challenges, and needs.

**Key capabilities:**
- **Smart recommendation engine:** 11 industries x 4 challenges x video options x 9 accessories = hundreds of unique configurations, all pre-mapped
- **Dual-audience output:** "For the Partner" (technical specs, product codes, talking points) and "For the Client" (benefit-focused, plain language) — switchable with one click
- **Auto-preselects accessories** by industry intelligence: selects Cold Chain for food companies, Panic Button for oil & gas, Rugged for construction — automatically
- **Existing vs. New client logic:** For existing customers, it only recommends upgrades and add-ons — doesn't re-sell what they already have
- **19 products** in the catalog with full specs, organized by category
- **Industry-specific talking points:** 44 unique sets of 4 talking points each — not generic bullets, but real competitive arguments per vertical
- **Partner branding:** Client-facing output shows the partner name as "Authorized Geotab Partner"
- **Full product catalog mode** for browsing without the wizard

**Business impact:** New partner sales reps used to take **weeks to learn the Geotab product portfolio**. Now they can walk into a meeting on Day 1 and recommend the right solution confidently. The dual-view output means they can **share a professional proposal with the client via WhatsApp in the meeting** — not days later.
